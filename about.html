get to know us

We Make Difference in their Life

The above-named Ministry is a non-governmental organization (NGO) (also known as Beauty for Ashes Ministry International (BFAMI)) headquartered in Maryland, United States. Her primary focus is to provide support to widows and their dependents in the different areas of need in their lives.
Over the last few years, God has used BFAWCM to be a blessing to the lives of widows financially, spiritually, emotionally and in many other ways as real-life issues and concerns pertaining to the widows are discussed and addressed. Increasingly, the widows are been helped to live peaceful, healthy, fulfilled and God-pleasing lives.
